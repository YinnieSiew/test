About MeowMeow U

Campus Cats is a fun and engaging platform for students to share their love for the cats roaming around campus. Here, you can upload photos or videos of cats you’ve spotted, create unique profiles for them, and interact with fellow cat enthusiasts.

Whether it’s giving a cat a name, voting in art and meme contests, or simply commenting on a cute post — this site is built for students to connect through their shared love of campus cats.

You can also report cat-related incidents (like stealing snacks or being a little too curious), and browse through searchable cat profiles to get to know your feline neighbors. Join the community, earn badges, and help create a cat-friendly campus environment!
